About

A technical profile grounded in delivery.

This site is designed to show the shape of my work rather than mirror my CV. I focus on data architectures, ontologies, governance, and reporting solutions that can support real workflows, especially in healthcare and research environments where clarity, traceability, and interoperability matter.

My background spans production healthcare systems, ontology engineering, research-led semantic tooling, and teaching in computing. I work comfortably from requirements analysis through schema design, validation rules, documentation, dashboards, and technical delivery.

That combination is useful when a project needs both deep structure and practical execution: data that is well modelled, systems that are maintainable, and outputs that support decisions rather than just storing information.

Services

Where I can add value.

My services are centered on the work I do best: structuring complex information, building usable systems around it, and helping organisations make the most of their data.

Data Modelling & Architecture

Schema design for relational and NoSQL systems, multi-module architecture planning, and structured data foundations for production software.

Ontology Engineering & Semantic Integration

Designing ontologies, aligning vocabularies, and creating semantic models that improve interoperability, reuse, and knowledge representation.

Healthcare Systems & EMR Design

Supporting clinical and administrative workflows through well-structured electronic medical record systems, validation rules, and reporting layers.

Data Governance & Standards

Data dictionaries, naming conventions, validation logic, and schema documentation that improve consistency and reduce downstream ambiguity.

Dashboards & Reporting

Operational dashboards and reporting workflows that turn data into decision support for monitoring, public health reporting, and management insight.

Teaching, Training & Technical Advisory

Workshops, curriculum support, guest teaching, and technical consultation in databases, semantic web, software engineering, and inclusive computing.

Selected Work

Projects that show the range of the work.

Healthcare Systems

Electronic Medical Records Platform

Semantic AI

LLM Ontology Alignment Tool

Developed a tool for uploading, analysing, and exporting ontology matches, supporting scalable semantic integration across domains.

Clinical Ontology

Designed a clinical ontology integrating SNOMED CT, ICD, and BFO, then published it through NCBO BioPortal to support interoperability and reuse.

Behavioural Science

Created ontology-driven tools and supporting infrastructure for behavioural science, including semantic integration, cross-domain reasoning, and scalable remote access.

Standards

IEEE 1872.1 Representation Work

Contributed an OWL ontology for robot task representation as part of an international working group focused on interoperability and semantic alignment.
